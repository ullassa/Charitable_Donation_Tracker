FAQs & support

Helpful answers for a smoother profile experience.

10 quick answers
Where does this profile data come from?

The page loads live data from the profile API and keeps the layout synced with the latest account details.

Can I update my profile information?

Yes. Customers can update the editable fields supported by the system and save changes securely.

Why is my address shown in a small card?

Address is intentionally compact to keep the profile clean while still keeping delivery details visible.

Is my data secure?

Yes. CareFund follows secure data handling with authentication and role-based access.

How do I contact support?

Use the chat option above or email support for faster assistance with account, donation, or payment issues.

Can I view donation history?

Donation history is available inside the customer dashboard for quick tracking and receipts.

What if my details are incorrect?

Update the editable fields and contact support if a field needs manual review.

Can charity managers update their profile?

Charity managers follow a separate verification flow and may need admin review for major updates.

How do I change password or security settings?

Use the login and security options from the account section once available in your dashboard.

Where can I find more features?

Explore dashboards, notifications, receipts, and charity pages to experience the full platform.
