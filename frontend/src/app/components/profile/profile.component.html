FAQs

Where does this data come from?

The page loads live data from the database through the profile API.

Can customer updates be saved?

Yes. Customer updates are saved back to the database and reloaded on refresh.

Can charity managers update their profile?

Yes. Charity managers can edit their charity record, including mission, about, activities, and contact details.
